About Me

I was born in Modesto, CA on July 14, 1990 but grew up in Casper, WY. I graduated from Kelly Walsh High School in 2008 and went on to pursue a biology degree at Colorado State University that fall. Three semesters later, I decided to change my major to geology, transfer to the University of Wyoming, and graduated in 2012 with my bachelors.


From there, I went to the Colorado School of Mines to get a masters degree. I completed that in 2014 and was hired on as a geologist at a petroleum company in Denver.
